SCHEDULE

This is an official SXSW event and requires a badge! The fun will take place in the Startup Village (located in the Hilton Hotel in Downtown Austin). All sessions will be in the Austin Grand Ballroom (Salon G) on the 6th Floor of the Hilton. The day will include 20 amazing speakers throughout five badass sessions.

LEARN & HAVE FUN

Clients are the difference between design and art.

MONDAY 16th
9:30 AM - 10:30 AM
What is Bitcoin?
A panel discussion covering the history of Bitcoin, what it is, & it’s implications as a global digital currency.
With Will O'Brien, Nic Cary, Stephen Pair, & Tatiana Moroz
11:00 AM - 12:00 AM
Bitcoin vs Altcoins
A panel discussion on how to improve Bitcoin including the role of sidechains, altcoins & exchanges.
With Vitalik Buterin, Joyce Kim, Charlie Lee, & Constance Choi
12:30 PM - 1:30 PM
A Future With Bitcoin
Four talks on topics that are relevant to Bitcoin becoming a part of mainstream culture and finance.
With Marco Santori, Jake Benson, Dan Elitzer, & Tina Hui
3:30 PM - 4:30 PM
Impact on Developing World
A panel discussion covering how Bitcoin is empowering the developing world through banking, charity, and more.
Sebastian Serrano, Connie Gallippi, Jonathan Zobro, & Sean Percival
15:15 PM - 15:45 PM
Real World Applications
A panel on why Bitcoin is a groundbreaking platform, what entrepreneurs are doing with it, & future technologies.
Vinny Lingham, Nick Sullivan, Adam Ludwin, & Adam Draper
